5 steps
to the perfect shave

Option 5 is an homage to the classic razors of the barbershop. Sleek precise and smooth, Option 5 aims to create a sense of success and tranquility in the shaving process.

step01

Huile de Rasage

Time stops as you add moisture and prep for a closer shave. The priming oil is meant to soften the skin and allow your shave to be as smooth and as safe as possible. Apply generously to allow for better foaming when adding the shave cream.

step02

brosse

Anticipation builds as you dunk your brush into warm water, shake off any excess. The brush is made of natural badger hair that will work to blend the priming oil and shave cream. Running it under some water will prepare it for much smoother application of the cream.

step03

Crème de Rasage

A drop of cream to the tip of your bush brings you one step closer. Now lather the areas you want shave. When lathering ,move in circular motions so as to properly mix the oil and the cream and prepare the skin for a smooth shave.

step04

Raser

It’s time to bring out the best you, Keep your shaver at a 30 angle to your face. The optimal angle for the razor to be effective.With the blade in hand you should enjoy the feeling of a swift and easy shave. The angle is just as important as the speed. Take your time and enjoy the experience.

Handle

step05

Après Rasage

The grand entrance, Bring back any lost moisture. By using the aftershave you allow for your skin to receive hydration. Be sure to wash off any excess shaving cream before applying. Your time is now. The World Awaits!
